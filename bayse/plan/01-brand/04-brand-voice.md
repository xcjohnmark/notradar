# 04 — Brand Voice & Tagline

> Status: 🟢 Defined — voice, tagline, and personality locked

## Tagline

**Primary tagline:** "Your edge on Bayse Markets."
**Short / Social bio:** "Your edge on Bayse." (abbreviated for tight spaces)

---

## Brand Positioning

Notradar is the **trading layer** built on top of Bayse Markets. It provides insights, analytics, and copy trading features that give users a competitive advantage. If Bayse Markets is the arena, Notradar is the playbook and the weapons.

---

## Brand Mission

To give every prediction market trader — from casual bettors to professional traders to degens — the edge they need to trade with conviction.

---

## Brand Personality

If Notradar were a person, they'd be: **The sharp, loud friend at the trading desk who's always got the alpha, talks shit when they're right, but backs it all up with receipts.** They're not corporate. They're not trying to be your advisor. They're the one who drops the call in the group chat before everyone else sees it — and they're not afraid to clown on you if you fade them.

### Personality Traits

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Provocative** | Challenges you to back your opinions with real positions. Doesn't let you sit on the sidelines. |
| **Degen-fluent** | Speaks the language of crypto Twitter, prediction markets, and trading culture natively. |
| **Bold** | Makes big claims and isn't afraid to be wrong loudly. Encourages conviction over hesitation. |
| **Memeable** | Content should be screenshot-worthy and shareable. If it can become a meme, even better. |
| **Alpha-driven** | Always focused on the edge, the insight, the information advantage. |

## Voice & Tone

### Voice (Always consistent)

| We are... | We are NOT... |
|-----------|---------------|
| Bold and provocative | Corporate or stiff |
| Degen-friendly and memeable | Cringe or trying too hard |
| Sharp and confident | Arrogant without substance |
| Community-first | Faceless or impersonal |
| Data-backed when it matters | Misleading or pump-and-dump |

### Tone (Adapts by context)

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| **Twitter / Marketing** | Provocative, memeable, bold | "You faded this at 12¢. It's at 89¢ now. Notradar users didn't. 📡" |
| **Product UI (labels)** | Clean, concise, sharp | "Top Movers" / "Copy This Trader" / "Your Edge" |
| **Onboarding** | Welcoming, hype, confident | "Welcome to the edge. Let's find your first trade." |
| **Winning notification** | Celebratory, flex energy | "Called it. 💰 +$42.50 on 'ETH above $4k'" |
| **Losing notification** | Honest, no sugarcoating, motivating | "Tough one. -$15.00. The market moved — recalibrate and reload." |
| **Error messages** | Direct, helpful, not cute | "Something broke. We're on it. Try again in a sec." |
| **Empty states** | Encouraging, action-oriented | "No positions yet. The markets are waiting — find your edge." |

## Writing Guidelines

### General Rules
- **Short sentences win.** If you can say it in 5 words, don't use 15.
- **Active voice always.** "You earned $50" not "A reward of $50 has been earned."
- **Use "you" liberally.** Make it personal. "Your portfolio" not "The portfolio."
- **Numbers are sacred.** Always use JetBrains Mono, always precise, never round unless the context demands it.
- **Capitalize Notradar.** Always. Never "notradar" in body text.

### Crypto/Trading Lingo — Use Freely
- Alpha, edge, conviction, position, fade, ape in
- LFG, WAGMI, probably nothing, ser
- Bullish, bearish, degen, diamond hands
- Don't overdo it — 1-2 per piece of copy, not every sentence

### Words We Don't Use
- "Gambling" (we're a trading/prediction platform)
- "Bet" as a noun in product UI (use "position" or "trade" — "bet" is fine in casual/social)
- "Risk-free" or "guaranteed" (never, ever)
- "Investment advice" (we don't give it, period)
