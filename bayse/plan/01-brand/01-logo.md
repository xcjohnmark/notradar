# 01 — Logo & Variations

> Status: 🟢 Primary logo selected and finalized

## Logo Direction (Decided)

| Decision | Choice |
|----------|--------|
| **Logo type** | Symbol / Icon (standalone icon, no wordmark in primary logo) |
| **Concept** | Radar / Signal waves forming a stylized "N" |
| **Style** | Cyber, high-tech, precise — clean lines suited for a terminal dashboard |
| **Orientation** | Centered, circular structure |
| **Color treatment** | Electric Cyan (primary), dark mode contrast |

### Design Brief

> The logo represents Notradar: a modern prediction market tracking and copytrading terminal.
> - **Stylized "N" & Radar/Signal Waves** sweeping outward in three distinct bands
> - **Electric Cyan** color accentuating the techy, data-driven visual style
> - Optimized for high visibility at small sizes (16px favicon) and dashboard headers

## Logo Variations Needed

- Sizes: 16px, 32px, 64px, 128px, 256px, 512px, 1024px

### Logotype (Text only)
- "NOTRADAR" or "Notradar" in brand font
- To be decided after typography selection

### Logo + Name (Horizontal Lockup)
- Notradar icon + "NOTRADAR" text side by side
- For headers, marketing materials

### Logo + Name (Stacked Lockup)
- Notradar icon on top, "NOTRADAR" text below
- For square formats, social avatars

---

## Logo Usage Rules

- **Minimum size:** 16px (icon only), 120px (with text)
- **Clear space:** At least 50% of the logo height on all sides
- **Approved backgrounds:** Dark (#0A0A0A), Light (#FFFFFF), brand gradient
- **Do NOT:**
  - Stretch or distort the logo
  - Change the logo colors outside of approved variations
  - Place on busy/low-contrast backgrounds
  - Add effects (drop shadows, outlines, glows)
  - Rotate the logo
