# Notradar — Brand Overview

## Platform
**Name:** Notradar
**Domain:** Prediction Markets (Sports, Crypto, Events)
**Visual Direction:** Dark terminal aesthetic (default), with light mode toggle
**Target Audience:** Prediction market users (Bayse Markets), degens/crypto Twitter, casual sports bettors, professional traders

---

## Brand Definition Roadmap

| Tier | What | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| **P0 — Ship Now** | Logo, color system, fonts, brand voice, tagline, favicon, OG images | Can't launch without these |
| **P1 — Launch Week** | Social templates, logo variations, dark/light tokens, icon style, data viz style | Needed for marketing & product |
| **P2 — Growth Phase** | Animations/GIFs, mascot, merch, stickers, community assets, meme templates | Builds culture & community |
| **P3 — Scale** | Full design system, co-branding guidelines, sound design, illustration library | Needed when team grows |
